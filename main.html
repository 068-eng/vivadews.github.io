About_Us

Vivadews is a subsidiary of O.J Brothers Nigeria Enterprises. A registered company in Nigeria since 1970. We are experienced with the Nigerian economy and we are known for our quality of service. We work to help Nigerians and by large Africans to achieve high value for every Kobo spent in their business or personal expenses. We are into sales/leasing of equipments, kitchen utensils, interior decoration and more. We are also into production of soaps and household cleaning agents. We take care of all your cleaning needs, whether post construction, business premises, swimming pool or after party. We are also very much involved in training. Our experienced and dedicated team is ever ready to assist you.
